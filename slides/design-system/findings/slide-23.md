# Slide 23 — The Attention Tracker
- **Archetype**: flow-diagram
- **Grid & layout**: Three-zone horizontal composition (slide 23). Left ~55%: a vertical stack of rounded "layer" capsules (a schematic transformer). Center: a lime bracket + connector text feeding the phrase "Focus Score". Right ~25% column: a vertical decision/threshold flow. A small footnote sits top-center-left above the diagram. Down-arrows top and bottom of the left stack imply the model continues beyond frame (省略). Full-width use of canvas; margins are tight.
- **Type roles**: head / body / mono-ish numerals. Ghost display title "Focus Score" top-left in very large low-contrast grey (the white-vs-grey title split — this is the greyed variant). A second "Focus Score" appears mid-right at medium size in white as the diagram's output label. Body CJK labels: connector "取注意力分數平均→", threshold captions "小於臨界值 / 大於臨界值", outcomes "攻擊，拒絕" and "正常，通過". Small numeric score chips (0.50, 0.55, 0.75, 0.90, 0.55) read as tabular/mono digits. Footnote is small grey CJK: "*為便於概念理解，本視覺化已省略 FFN, MLP Residual Stream 等機制結構" mixing CJK with Latin acronyms.
- **Color / tokens**: Near-black background. Nodes are grey filled circles arranged in rows inside pill-shaped layer capsules. Lime accent is load-bearing: highlighted attention nodes are lime-filled chips carrying the score numbers, and a lime bracket/connector routes selected nodes to the "取注意力分數平均" step (slide 23). Outcome colors follow the semantic pair: "攻擊，拒絕" in purple/violet, "正常，通過" in green. Threshold captions and arrows are grey. No viridis ramp.
- **Components present**: (1) Layer capsule — a horizontal pill containing a row of grey token-circles with a leading "…" ellipsis; several stacked with vertical "⋮" gaps to imply many layers. (2) Attention-node highlight — lime circle chip with a numeric score. (3) Lime aggregation bracket + arrow connector labeled "取注意力分數平均→". (4) Output label "Focus Score" (white). (5) Right-side threshold flow: outcome (purple) ↑ / "小於臨界值" / vertical arrow / "Focus Score" pivot / "大於臨界值" / outcome (green). (6) Rotated vertical axis labels on the far left marking shallow→deep layers. (7) Top/bottom down-arrows. No lesson-style capsule cards here.
- **Motion hints**: Strong staged-build feel — nodes could light up (lime) one row at a time, the bracket then draws to the average, then the threshold flow branches up (reject) vs down (pass). The dual up/down arrows off "Focus Score" invite an animated compare-to-threshold reveal.
- **Reusable pattern**: This slide is an instance of "an annotated schematic diagram that routes highlighted data points through an aggregation step into a binary threshold decision."
- **One-offs / inconsistencies**: The giant grey ghost "Focus Score" wordmark is a decorative/watermark treatment that duplicates the white output label — likely a deliberate title-echo, but note it's the only place a title and a content label share identical text at two sizes/tiers. Vertical rotated layer labels appear only here in the range.
